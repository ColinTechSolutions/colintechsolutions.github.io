Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 1–3 business days, depending on the issue and parts availability.
Do you offer on-site services?
No, we do not provide on-site services. All support is offered remotely or via local drop-off and pickup.
Can I bring my custom PC for upgrades?
Yes! We offer upgrades for custom PCs, including RAM, storage, GPU, cooling, and more.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, and mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Do I need an appointment to drop off my device?
Yes, all drop-offs and pickups are by appointment only. We do not operate a storefront, but offer scheduled local meetups in designated public locations.
Is there a diagnostic fee?
Yes, diagnostic services typically range from $30 to $50 depending on the complexity of the issue. This fee is waived if you approve the recommended repair service.
Can you help recover lost or deleted files?
Yes, we offer file recovery services for accidentally deleted or lost data from hard drives, SSDs, and USB drives. While recovery success depends on how the device was used after the data loss, we'll do our best using advanced tools to retrieve your important files. Please note: severely damaged or failing drives may require professional lab recovery.
What types of devices do you repair?
We repair laptops, desktop PCs, and custom builds. If you’re unsure whether we can assist with your device, please contact us with the details — we’re happy to help!
Do you offer remote support for software issues?
Yes, we provide secure remote support sessions for software troubleshooting, system cleanup, and configuration help.
